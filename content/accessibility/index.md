## Our Commitment

This website is committed to meeting the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 at Level AA. We want every visitor — regardless of ability, device, or assistive technology — to be able to access the information and services offered here.

## What We Do

- Use clear, plain language throughout the site
- Maintain proper heading structure for screen readers
- Provide descriptive link text
- Ensure sufficient color contrast between text and background
- Support keyboard navigation on all pages
- Use semantic HTML for assistive technology compatibility
