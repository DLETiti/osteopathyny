## What is the ALF Appliance?

The ALF is a lightweight, removable dental appliance designed to gently guide the development and alignment of the jaw and facial structures. Unlike traditional orthodontic approaches that focus primarily on straightening teeth, the ALF works with the body's natural growth patterns to address underlying structural and functional issues.
